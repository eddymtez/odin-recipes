Lasagna

Description

This is a tasty lasagna dish that anyone can make.

Very simple recipe with basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 32oz jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F degrees.
  2. In large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Cook onion in skillet for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until meat begins to brown (approximately 5 minutes)
  5. Sprinkle with garlic powder, salt and pepper
  6. Lower heat to low and add marinara sauce and allow to simmer
  7. In a bowl, stir together ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, egg, basil, salt and pepper
  8. Cook lasagna noodles as instructed on packaging
  9. In baking pan, spread 1/4 cup of meat sauce. Place 3 noodles on top. Add cheese mixture. Repeat as necessary
  10. Bake for 15 minutes. Continue to bake until cheese is bubbling.
