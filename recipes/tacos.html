Tacos

Description

Tacos. What else is there to say?

If you mess up this recipe, then I don't know what to tell you.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Meat of some kind
  • Some kind of garnish like salsa, lime, diced onions or cilantro

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Heat up the tortillas
  3. Put the cooked meat in the tortillas
  4. Add any garnishes you want
