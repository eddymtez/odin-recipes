Green Enchiladas

Description

This is a tasty, simple, and authentic enchilada recipe.

Impress your friends with this one simple enchilada recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken (roughly 2lbs of cooked chicken meat)
  • 8 oz monterey jack cheese (grated)
  • 8 oz cheddar cheese (grated)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups green enchilada sauce
  • 10 corn tortillas
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F degrees
  2. Shred chicken meat into a bowl (doesn't have to be too finely shredded)
  3. Add cumin, salt, hot sauce, and half of each of the cheeses to the chicken and mix
  4. Coat the bottom of a casserole dish with a small amount of enchilada sauce
  5. In a dry pan, heat up a tortilla for a few seconds. Add 1/10 of the chicken mixture to a tortilla
  6. Once all 10 tortillas are in the casserole dish, add the enchilada sauce. Cover with the remaining cheese
  7. Bake for 40 minutes
